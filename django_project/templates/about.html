📰 About Us - NepNews

Your Source. Your Story.

At NepNews, we believe that news isn't just about headlines—it's about people, voices, and the stories that shape our world. Founded with a mission to redefine how news is delivered in Nepal and beyond, NepNews is your trusted source for timely, transparent, and transformative journalism.

💡 Who We Are

We are a dynamic team of journalists, developers, storytellers, and visionaries driven by a common goal: to empower people with accurate information. We’re not just a news portal—we’re a platform for conversations, perspectives, and progress.

🎯 Our Mission

To deliver reliable, unbiased, and in-depth news while embracing the power of technology and innovation. We aim to bridge the gap between the newsmakers and news seekers, ensuring that every voice is heard.

From breaking national headlines to inspiring local stories, from business trends to sports highlights, we bring you a curated mix of updates that inform and inspire.
